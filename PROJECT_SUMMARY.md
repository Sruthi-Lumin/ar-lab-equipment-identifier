## ğŸ”¬ Scientific Features

### Bayesian Recognition

Uses Bayes' theorem to improve detection accuracy:

1. **Prior Probability**: Initial belief in equipment type
2. **Likelihood**: How likely is detection given equipment
3. **Posterior Probability**: Updated belief after observation
4. **History Tracking**: Temporal reasoning from past detections

### Safety Focus

Every equipment entry includes:
- Specific safety warnings
- Operational guidelines
- Best practices
- Common mistakes to avoid
